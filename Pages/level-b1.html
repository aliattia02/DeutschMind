💡 B1 Learning Tips

🎯 Think in German

Challenge yourself to think in German for 5 minutes a day. Start with simple thoughts: 'Ich bin müde', 'Das Wetter ist schön'.

🔊 Watch German TV

Try German series with German subtitles. Start with shows for teens — they use clear, everyday German!

🎨 Master Word Order

German word order is key at B1! Practice with subordinate clauses: 'weil', 'dass', 'wenn', 'obwohl'.

✍️ Write Paragraphs

Write 2-3 paragraphs about topics you know. Connect sentences using 'weil', 'aber', 'deshalb', 'trotzdem'.

🗣️ Find a Language Partner

Practice speaking with a native or fellow learner. iTalki and Tandem are great for finding partners!

📅 Set SMART Goals

Set specific goals: 'I will learn 20 new words this week' rather than 'I will study German'.
