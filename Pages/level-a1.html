💡 A1 Learning Tips

🎯 Focus on Basics

Master the most common 200–300 words first. They cover 60% of everyday conversations!

🔊 Listen Daily

Spend 15 minutes daily listening to German. Use the audio features on every page!

🎨 Use Colors

Our color system helps: Der (Blue), Das (Green), Die (Red). Visual memory is powerful!

✍️ Practice Writing

Write simple sentences using new vocabulary. Start a German diary with 3 sentences per day!

🗣️ Speak Aloud

Don't be shy! Repeat after the audio. Speaking helps your brain remember better!

📅 Consistency > Intensity

30 minutes daily is better than 3 hours once a week. Make it a habit!
